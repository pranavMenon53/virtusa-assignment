About Us


The CVR College of Engineering was established in 2000. It is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education and it is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in engineering and technology. It also has research programmes in selected fields. At the undergraduate level, B. Tech programmes are available for the areas of- Civil, Mechanical, Computer Science, Electronics & Instrumentation, Information Technology (IT), Electronics & Communication and Electrical & Electronics. At the master’s level, it offers M.Tech in VSLI Design, Artificial Intelligence, Structural Engineering, Embedded Systems, Data Sciences and Electrical Power Engineering. Location: CVR College of Engineering is located in Mangalpally(V), Ibrahimpatnam(M), Ranga Reddy. It is 20 km from the centre of Hyderabad. It is about 34 km away from the Secunderabad Railway Junction, and about 25 km away from the Rajiv Gandhi International Airport. The nearest bus depot is about 10 km away from the college. The place is accessible by all transportation.

Infrastructure: A Key Element of Students

The College is spread over a built-up area of over 36,000 sqm. on a sprawling 33-acre campus amidst verdant surroundings.
The College has

  • 70 spacious classrooms and 31 labs
  • An independent three storied Library building
  • An independent four storied Student Centre
  • Auditorium
  • Extensive outdoor sports facilities
  • Hostel facilities
